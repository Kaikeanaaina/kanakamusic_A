What genres of songs does KanakaMusic have?

KanakaMusic focus on all music created or commonly listened in Hawaii, but we categorized songs or artists by Hawaiian or Contemporary. If majority of the song is in Hawaiian, it will be categorized as a Hawaiian song. If majority of the song is in any other language, it will be categorized as a contemporary song. If the artist recorded mostly Hawaiian songs, the artist is categorized as a Hawaiian artist, all else are categorized as a contemporary artist
